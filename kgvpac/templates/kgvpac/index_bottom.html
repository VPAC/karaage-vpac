About V3

Staff and students employed or enrolled at any of V3’s Member Universities may gain access to V3’s HPC Facilities through their University subscription program.

Access may also be provided to Industry, however, is subject to an appropriate arrangement with V3. Interested persons should contact help@vpac.org.

As a Member of V3, each University has a pre-determined share of V3’s High Performance Computing capacity. Each University permits their share to be used by researchers and students conducting approved research consistent with the University's policies.

A staff or student who wishes to apply for an account will require a personal account and must be associated with a University approved Project. All Projects are approved by the respective University’s Operations Committee Member.

Upon approval, authority is provided to the nominated Project Leader to authorise personal accounts associated with the Project.

Acceptable Use Policy
